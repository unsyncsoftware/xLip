Frequently Asked Questions
Do I need a credit card to start?
No. The free plan and 30-day Pro trial require no payment details at all.
What happens when my trial ends?
You'll get an email 2 days before your trial ends. After 15 days you automatically move back to guest mode — no charges ever without your consent.
Why are prices different in different countries?
We use Purchasing Power Parity (PPP) pricing — the same idea used by companies like Notion and Figma. We want xlip.uk to be accessible to everyone, not just people in wealthy countries.
What is Pro Max?
Pro Max is for power users and small teams who need more links, deeper analytics, custom domains, and branded links. Think of it as Pro — but maxed out.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. Cancel anytime from your dashboard — no questions asked. You keep access until the end of your billing period.
Do you show ads on my short links?
Never. Not on free, not on paid. xlip.uk is ad-free forever. Unlike some competitors who show full-page ads before redirecting — we think that's disrespectful to your audience.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit/debit cards via Stripe, and PayPal. More payment options coming soon.
