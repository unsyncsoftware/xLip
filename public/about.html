The Story

xlip.uk started as a personal project — a simple tool to make long, ugly URLs shareable. What began as a weekend experiment quickly turned into something worth building properly.

It's built and maintained by a solo developer from the Philippines, under the indie software umbrella of UnsyncSoftware.com. No VC funding. No big team. Just one person who believes the internet deserves better tools — free, fast, and without the bloat.

xlip.uk is proof that you don't need a Silicon Valley team to build something useful. Sometimes all you need is a good idea, a cheap server, and too much free time.

What We Do

xlip.uk is a free URL shortener that turns long, unwieldy links into short, clean ones. No account required to get started — paste your URL, get a short link, done.

For power users, we offer custom aliases, password-protected links, QR code generation, and click analytics — all available after a free account registration.
